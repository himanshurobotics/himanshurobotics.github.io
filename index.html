  1. Non-linear Fractional Order Fuzzy PD Plus I Controller for Trajectory Optimization of 6-DOF Modified Puma-560 Robotic Arm
    Himanshu Varshney , Jyoti Yadav , and Himanshu Chhabra
    In Proceedings of Congress on Control, Robotics, and Mechatronics, Smart Innovation, Systems and Technologies (Springer) , 2023
    Abs

    The purpose of this research is to employ non-integer order calculus to enhance the control action of the non-linear fractional order fuzzy PD plus I (FOFPD\thinspace+\thinspaceI) controller. To operate a non-linear 6-DOF Puma 560 robotic arm, a FOFPD\thinspace+\thinspaceI controller is developed and implemented. Fractional order fuzzy proportional derivative (FOFPD) and fractional order fuzzy integral (FOFI) controllers are used to create the proposed controller. Because of the non-linear gains, the proposed control approach preserves the linear structure of the fractional order proportional derivative plus integral (FOPD\thinspace+\thinspaceI) controller while still providing adaptive capabilities. Further, the PID controller is also derived to compare with the FOFPD\thinspace+\thinspaceI controller. Both, FOFPD\thinspace+\thinspaceI and PID controller’s parameters are optimized using non-dominated sorting genetic algorithm-II (NSGA-II). The performance and effectiveness of the presented controller are examined in terms of trajectory tracking, tracking error, and robotic arm control efforts. Simulation shows excellent performance of FOFPD\thinspace+\thinspaceI controller over traditional PID and fuzzy logic controllers. To be precise, on the current optimal solution, suggested controlling strategy is more than 80% efficient than other strategies.
